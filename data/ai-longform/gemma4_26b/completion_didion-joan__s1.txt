The technical guys, they had their instruments, of all. But we, the observers, we were there for something else. We were there to witness the spectacle. I remember one night on Johnston, just before we moved up to the Aleutians. It wasn't just the tests, you see. It was the whole era. Everything felt like it was on the verge of something.
