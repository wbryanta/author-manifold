They were sitting in a restaurant in Hong Kong. This would have been 1969, or possibly 1970. Jack Lovett's inclination, Inez had long since determined, was to see the abalone shell. Her husband Harry Victor's inclination, had Harry Victor been present, would have been to talk about the spilled fuel, the specific gravity of the spill, the culpability of certain parties, the systemic failures that led to spills of this kind, the children who would suffer the downstream effects.
